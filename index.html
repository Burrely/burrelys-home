The Journey - What to expect when commissioning me

When you commission me I'll take you with me through the creation of your product. This includes payment, process and feedback. Below I have summed up what this journey will look like and what you can expect:

  1. You contact me on Discord with details regarding your request (such as:)
    • A general description.
    • Features you'd like your product to have.
    • Reference material such as refsheets, art, moodboards etc.
    • Which files you want and how you'd like them to be formatted. (think of .unitypackages, zip files containing textures etc.)
  2. I either accept or decline your request
  3. I'll create a commission sheet
    • I'll ask you for an e-mail which will be used to grant you access to the commission sheet, this e-mail will also be used to grant you access to the final product after the final payment.
    • If any questions arise I'll ask you for more details.
  4. You'll be sent an invoice based on 50% of the estimate provided in the commission sheet
    • This payment will be used to kickstart the commission and acts as a promise.
    • Depending on whether your project requires 3rd party assets I might have to ask you to provide them to me.1 Currently for Gumroad I only accept gifted products due to their Paypal debacle.
  5. I start working on your commission
    • I'll keep regular contact with you in regards to the project.
      • Updates on the project
      • specifics on certain features
      • Scope / Budgetary issues2
  6. I finalize the project
    • I'll send you some more updates and ask whether you are happy with the product.
  7. You'll be sent an invoice based on the remainder of the project costs
    • This payment will be used as a safeguard to make sure I'll get paid for my work.
    • The remainder is ((hours spent on the project x hourly rate) - initial payment).3
  8. You'll be sent the files over Google Drive using the e-mail provided
1 - If I already own the required assets or the asset doesn't require me as an artist to purchase a separate copy then there is no further cost attached to this. I'll make sure to make you aware if this is the case.
2 - I'll always try to account for unexpected issues during the creation of the estimate but it can happen that we run short regardless, if this is the case I'll contact you and we can talk about either scrapping features, adjusting features or expanding the budget.
3 - If the initial payment is higher than the total then your project will be considered paid and this step will be considered redundant as well.

TOS (Terms of Services)

My TOS tries to be reasonable towards both you and me.

If you have any objections, questions or wishes regarding these then please let me know before sending information regarding your request.

  • Rights to reference & source materials
    • If I'm given reference material by you for a project, I'm given permission to use this for the purposes of said project for the duration of the project until either project completion or termination.
    • I reserve all rights to the project until the final price has been paid in full.1
  • Refusal of Services
    • I have the right to refuse and terminate a project at any given time for any given reason.2
    • I will only provide my services to people who are 18 years of age or older. If you are under this age you can ask a parent or a guardian to communicate and request my services in your stead.
    • When your project gets terminated any financial investment you've made will also be forfeit (see Refunds).
  • Refunds
    • I do not offer refunds due to the nature of my work (service). I charge by time, not by product.
  • Product rights
    • The final product may be used for games, prints and similar commercial purposes. However, digital copies of the product may not be sold- whether independently or in a premade pack (example: kitbashes).3
    • You are not allowed to turn the product or depictions of the product into a or multiple Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs).
    • You are allowed to share temporary product rights to the final product with other artists for the purpose of further modification.4 5
    • You are allowed to sell my work to another individual or entity, however, this third party will also have to accept these Terms of Services.6 7
  • Credits
    • If you include a credits page in your work (in games/videos for example) I would like to be credited for my work.
    • I won't force any credits on the product, instead I'd like to ask you to credit me by word of mouth when asked about my creations.
1 - These rights may include the rights to the likeness of reference material due to the product being a derivative of said reference.
2 - The most prominent reasons for refusal of services are, but are not limited to: Harassment, threats, spam, doxxing, disrespect, refusal of payment.
3 - I currently do not offer commercial licenses yet, but if you have any specific questions regarding this you can let me know in your request.
4 - In the case of kitbashes or instances where 3rd party assets are being utilized, the TOS of their creators still apply, I cannot grant you any rights over their property that they haven't granted you.
5 - The temporary rights granted by you may not be transfered to other people by the people you are granting it to.
6 - Transactions of the product may be made without my involvement, however you are required to update me on the ownership of the product and rights with clear contact details when ownership changes.
7 - Selling the product implies that you lose the rights to the product and any copies of it. This includes the rights to grant temporary rights to third parties.
